# PWHL Fantasy League Rules v1

## Overview

PWHL Fantasy is a season-long fantasy hockey game built around the Professional Women's Hockey League (PWHL).

Managers draft players, set weekly lineups, compete in head-to-head matchups, and earn Victory Points (VP) through both matchup results and league-wide scoring performance.

The goal is to finish near the top of the standings and qualify for the fantasy playoffs.

---

# League Philosophy

PWHL Fantasy is designed for:

- PWHL fans
- Casual fantasy players
- First-time fantasy managers

The platform follows an opinionated, easy-to-learn approach similar to ESPN and Yahoo Fantasy Sports.

---

# League Size

Recommended league size:

- 8 teams

Supported league sizes:

- 8–10 teams

The default experience is balanced around an 8-team league.

---

# Season Format

The fantasy season mirrors the PWHL regular season.

The fantasy season consists of:

1. Draft
2. Regular Season
3. Fantasy Playoffs
4. Fantasy Championship

Fantasy playoffs conclude before the start of the PWHL playoffs.

---

# Draft

## Draft Type

Snake Draft

Draft order reverses each round.

Example:

Round 1:
1 → 8

Round 2:
8 → 1

Round 3:
1 → 8

## Draft Timer

Default draft timer:

- 90 seconds per selection

## Auto Draft

If a manager fails to make a pick before time expires, the highest-ranked available player will be selected automatically.

# Lineup Management

## Weekly Lineup Lock

Lineups lock at the beginning of each matchup week.

Managers may make lineup adjustments during the week only for players who have not yet played a game during the current matchup period.

Once a player has played a game during the current scoring period, that player's lineup slot is locked for the remainder of the week.

This allows managers to react to injuries and schedule changes while preserving the strategic value of weekly lineup decisions.

# Victory Points (VP)

PWHL Fantasy uses a hybrid standings system called Victory Points (VP).

This system rewards both:

- Winning your weekly matchup
- Being one of the highest-scoring teams in the league

## Matchup VP

| Result | VP Awarded |
|----------|------------|
| Win | 2 VP |
| Tie | 1 VP |
| Loss | 0 VP |

## Weekly Scoring Bonus VP

After all teams have completed the matchup week:

| Weekly Score Rank | Bonus VP |
|-------------------|-----------|
| Highest Score in League | +2 VP |
| Second Highest Score in League | +1 VP |

If multiple teams tie for the highest weekly score, all tied teams receive the First Place bonus.

Second Place VP is awarded only when a unique first-place score exists.

## Weekly Maximum

A team can earn a maximum of:

- 4 VP in a single week

(2 VP for a matchup win + 2 VP for highest weekly score)

---

# Standings

Standings are ordered by total accumulated Victory Points.

## Tiebreakers

If teams are tied in VP:

1. Matchup wins
2. Head-to-head results
3. Total fantasy points scored
4. Commissioner determination

---

# Waivers

PWHL Fantasy uses a rolling waiver system.

Players dropped from a roster enter waivers before becoming free agents.

Managers with higher waiver priority receive preference on successful claims.

FAAB (Free Agent Acquisition Budget) is not part of Version 1.

---

# Trades

Trades are permitted during the regular season.

## Trade Review

Commissioners may review and approve trades.

The commissioner reserves the right to reverse trades determined to be collusive or detrimental to league integrity.

## Trade Deadline

The trade deadline occurs prior to the fantasy playoffs.

---

# Playoffs

## Qualification

For the standard 8-team league:

- Top 4 teams qualify

Seeds are determined by regular season Victory Point standings.

## Seeding

| Seed | Qualification |
|--------|---------------|
| 1 | Highest VP total |
| 2 | Second Highest VP total |
| 3 | Third Highest VP total |
| 4 | Fourth Highest VP total |

## Format

Single-elimination bracket.

### Semifinals

- #1 vs #4
- #2 vs #3

### Championship

Semifinal winners advance to the Fantasy Championship.

---

# Stat Corrections

Official PWHL stat corrections may result in score adjustments after games conclude.

Stat corrections may affect:

- Weekly matchup outcomes
- Weekly VP totals
- League standings
- Playoff qualification

Official stat corrections are final.

---

# Commissioner Powers

Commissioners may:

- Manage league membership
- Approve or reject trades
- Pause drafts
- Resume drafts
- Correct league errors
- Replace inactive managers

All commissioner actions are logged by the platform.

---

# Sportsmanship

Managers are expected to:

- Maintain competitive rosters
- Participate in good faith
- Avoid collusion
- Respect other league members

The commissioner may take action when league integrity is threatened.
