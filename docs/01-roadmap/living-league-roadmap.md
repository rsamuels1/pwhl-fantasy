PWHL GM — Living League Roadmap

Delight Mechanics v1

⸻

Vision

PWHL GM should feel like following a living hockey season.

Managers should experience:

• Competition

• Anticipation

• Recognition

• Legacy

• League history

• Storytelling

Success means managers open the app not only to set lineups, but to see what happened around the league.

⸻

Milestone 1 — Weekly Delight

1. Weekly Awards Ceremony

User Story

As a manager,

I want to receive league awards every Tuesday,

So that exceptional performances feel recognized.

Acceptance Criteria

System automatically generates awards after matchup finalization.

Awards may include:

🏆 Ice-Cold Closer

🔥 Heater Award

💀 Heartbreaker

📉 Collapse of the Week

🧊 Frozen Stick

Awards appear:

• Home page

• Notifications

• Weekly recap page

⸻

2. Matchup Momentum Strip

User Story

As a manager,

I want to see momentum shifts during live matchups,

So that games feel exciting even when I am behind.

Acceptance Criteria

Momentum card displays:

🔥 Momentum

+9.7 points since yesterday

3 players remaining

Opponent finished

Hidden before games start

Collapses after matchup completion

Appears beneath matchup header

North Star

PWHL GM should feel like walking through an arena concourse between periods and seeing headlines, trophies, standings, rivalries, and history unfolding around you.

It should never feel like configuring accounting software.
