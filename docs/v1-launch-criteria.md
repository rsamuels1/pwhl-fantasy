# V1 Launch Criteria

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

Owner: Product

Purpose: Define the minimum feature set required for public launch and prevent scope creep.

---

# Launch Philosophy

A successful launch is:

- Reliable
- Understandable
- Operable

A successful launch is not:

- Feature complete

# Launch Readiness Checklist

All must be true.

---

## Product

- League creation works
- Draft works
- VP works
- Playoffs work

---

## Engineering

- No unresolved P0 issues
- MVP validation suite passing
- Season simulation passing

---

## Operations

- Founding commissioners recruited
- Feedback process established
- Support process documented

---

## Beta

- Draft completion ≥ 90%
- Week 1 participation ≥ 80%
- Season completion ≥ 75%

---

# Definition of Launch Ready

The product is launch ready when a league can successfully complete:

Create League
→ Invite Managers
→ Draft
→ Weekly Matchups
→ VP Standings
→ Playoffs
→ Champion

without administrator intervention.
