The road to a public beta. Seven phases, twenty-five features, one live fantasy season.

The flagship experience is the live fantasy season — drafting real players, setting weekly lineups, and competing in matchups scored from real PWHL games. Here's everything shipped, in progress, and still ahead.

What to build next

The highest-value gaps on the path to a public beta, in priority order.
  1. Lineup Management v2 · #24
    High value, near-zero new dependencies. Fixes a real engagement/UX gap before beta.
  2. League Onboarding Wizard · #2
    Completely unbuilt — the biggest blocker for self-serve signups.
  3. Mobile Optimization · #3
    Responsive draft room + matchup screens; also unblocks #24's mobile goal.
  4. Error Handling · #4
    Empty and loading states across the app.
  5. Trade System + Transaction History · #7 · #8
    The missing half of league management.
  6. Team Analysis & Insights · #25
    Engagement differentiator — ship analysis + free-agent half now, trades later.
  7. Waiver Priority + Processing · #5
    Upgrade immediate add/drop into a real waiver wire.
Stretch (not beta blockers): league-wide matchup storylines (#11) and the rivalry / Hall-of-Fame retention layer (#17–#18). Replay work stays out of this list — invest only when it unblocks testing.
